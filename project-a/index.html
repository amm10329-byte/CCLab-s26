The Pest

Scientific Name:

optotermes formosanus

Discovered by / at:

Discovered in 2026, by a person named John Smith. It was reported this creature was caught eating the silverware in his drawer.

Family:

This creature is part of the Termite family, evolved separately to not only eat wood, but any and everything it sees. While the creature is small, it will stop at nothing until its full.

Habitat:

Found in forests and fields. Usually reports are from people who live on Farms or in Cabins.

Appearance:

The creature changes color based on its surroundings. When found in grassy areas, the creature turns a darkish green color. While moving, the creature has red spikes that potrude from its body, warding off predators. The creatures eyes are very human like, giving it offputting appearance.

Key Characteristics:

  • Camoflauge.
  • Eats everything.
  • Red Spikes
  • Human-like Eyes

What we know so far about The Pest? We know that the Pest enjoys hiding in the shadows, and feeds on whatever is near it. It has a seemingly everlasting hunger, and will do whatever it takes to eat when hungry. The Pest is also very quick. When you try to stomp on it, or capture it, it quickly avoids you.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature We can interact with the creature by moving the mouse around, acting as a flashlight, to reveal it. Another method is to click, which resembles you placing food on the ground. Once you click the first time, the creature goes to the mouse, and acts erratically. You can try and click on the pest again to capture it, but it will evade you and go back into hiding into the shadows.
